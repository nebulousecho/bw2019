Application Guide

Eligibility

If you're proud of the workplace you've created, take a few minutes to apply and let Inc. Magazine declare your company one of the best places to work in America.

To participate in the 2018 Inc. Best Workplaces award, your company must meet each of the following qualifications:

  • Be privately-owned
  • Independent (not a subsidiary or division of another company)
  • Based in the United States
  • Have at least 10 employees

There are a few limited exceptions to the requirements. Please call our team with any questions about eligibility at 212-389-5505.

Why Apply?

For nearly 40 years, Inc. magazine has been devoted to recognizing America's most dynamic businesses and honoring the great things they do. Last year, nearly 250 companies made the cut and were honored in print or online.

2018 honorees of Inc.'s Best Workplaces will enjoy the following benefits:

  • National Recognition: Be honored as an employer of choice, and elevate your firm's employer brand.
  • Visibility & Media Exposure: Be featured in the June issue of Inc. Magazine (select companies), get a dedicated company profile on Inc.com, and receive national and local coverage from the likes of Yahoo! Finance and Businesswire.
  • Greater Productivity: Drive engagement and enthusiasm of current employees while sending a clear message that the culture of your company is a primary strategic priority. Improve recruiting opportunities with prospective employees.
  • Invaluable Insights: Learn what your employees are really thinking with a company overview filled with valuable information you can use to improve your day-to-day operations and workplace environment.
  • One-Year Subscription: As part of your registration you will receive a one-year subscription to Inc. magazine, the leading business publication with news, tools, and advice to help you succeed.

FAQ

Why should my company apply?

For nearly 40 years, Inc. magazine has been devoted to recognizing America's most dynamic businesses and honoring the great things they do. Recognition as an employer of choice will elevate your firm's employer brand and improve recruiting. It will drive engagement and enthusiasm of current employees. And it will send a clear message that the culture of your company is a primary strategic priority.

What will I receive?

If your company is rated one of the Best Workplaces, you will receive mention on Inc.com and the honor will be highlighted on your Inc.com company profile. You will also be considered for coverage in Inc. magazine. Win or lose, you'll receive an Overview Report, based on your employee survey, which will show your company's score on key measurements, including how your leadership views its employees and whether there is a gender gap in opportunity at your company.

Is there a cost to participate?

Yes. There is a non-refundable processing fee for all applicants. The application fee is $125. Identifying the best workplaces in the U.S. is an enormous editorial project that, among other things, raises awareness of entrepreneurship as a valuable force in our economy. The processing fee covers only a portion of the operational costs of this undertaking. All participating companies will receive an Overview Report, which shows how your company scored in several categories. Quantum Workplace offers the following features for an additional cost:
  1. If your employees do not have access to a computer, paper surveys are available for a nominal fee ($2 per submitted paper survey).
  2. You may add questions or demographics to the survey to enhance your survey results. Fees for customized surveys vary, depending on the number of employees. (Only standard survey questions will be used in scoring for the competition.)
  3. Participants may purchase a package of their survey results. Prices for packages are based on the size of the company.

How are the Inc. Best Places to Work honorees determined?

All of Inc.'s Best Workplaces honorees are determined primarily on the basis of their employees' responses. Employee responses to the 30 standard survey questions, the number of employees who respond to the survey, along with an audit of the organization's benefits are used to determine the company's overall score and ranking. Quantum Workplace is a leading provider of employee feedback software and analytics.

Can my company customize the employee survey?

Yes, you may add questions or demographics to the survey to enhance your survey results. Fees for customized surveys vary. Please contact Kristin Pikop, kristin@quantumworkplace.com, for more information about custom surveys. Customizations do not affect the way your results are scored for the purpose of the competition. Custom questions and non-market employees are excluded from the calculations.

How is the survey distributed to my employees?

The contact person at your company will facilitate the survey invitations for your employees. There are several options available for distribution.
  • Email employees the unique survey link by uploading employee email addresses into the Quantum Workplace portal.
  • Employees without an email address can be invited by hand delivering them a unique survey link.
  • Paper surveys are available to employees without access to a computer. Please note that there is a nominal fee to process paper surveys and the paper survey deadline precedes the online survey deadline.

Should partners or owners be allowed to take the survey?

No, partners and owners with more than 5 percent ownership in the company should not be invited to complete the survey.

Should union employees be included?

Yes, union employees must be included in your employee count and invited to take the survey.

Is the survey confidential?

The Quantum Workplace employee survey is strictly confidential. Companies will have access to survey results at the aggregate level to help you understand and build engagement. Note that certain verbatims will be included as part of the snapshot report, but without attribution (entirely anonymous).

Do all of the employees at my company need to complete the survey?

All eligible employees must be invited to take the survey. To reach a 95 percent confidence level with a margin of error of plus or minus 5 percent, you will need to achieve employee participation based on the following percentages per size of organization for your company to be considered an honoree:
  • 50 employees or fewer = 85%
  • 51 - 150 = 70%
  • 151 - 500 = 50%
  • 501 - 1,000 = 30%
  • 1,001 - 3,000 = 15%
  • 3,001 - 5,000 = 8%
  • More than 5,000 = 5%

Most of my employees work part time. Can I include my part-time employees?

Part-time employees should be included in your employee count.

My company has offices in multiple cities and states. How many employees should I enter at the registration page?

Please include all permanent employees (full- and part-time) who work within the United States. If you would like to include all employees working outside of the United States, contact Kristin Pikop at 402.819.4709 or kristin@quantumworkplace.com to discuss customization options and pricing. Only responses from employees in the U.S. will be considered for the contest.

Will my company receive the survey results?

Yes. You will receive an Overview Report, which will show how your company scored in several categories. You will also have the opportunity to purchase in-depth survey results.

What if my company does not make Inc.'s Best Workplaces list?

You will still receive access to your Overview report, and you will have the opportunity to purchase reports to help improve employee engagement. Your company name will not be placed on any list and will not be printed in the special publication.

How will I know if my company made the list? When will I be contacted?

Companies that have reached their participation goal will be contacted regarding their status as a finalist or non-finalist by mid-May.

When will the list of honorees be published?

The names of top-performing workplaces will be published in Inc. magazine's June issue. If your company is not honored on the list, your participation remains 100 percent confidential. For honorees, selected answers could be used in editorial content. You would likely be contacted by Inc. magazine writers or fact checkers.

When can I access my company's survey results?

You may access results after you receive your status notification by mid-May.

What if I have questions that are not listed here?
